About the Master Guide

This is one of the most current leadership development program for youth ministry in the Seventh-day Adventist Church. The Master Guide is the foundation for youth ministry leadership, then builds two levels of continuing education that will help keep youth leaders sharp.

  • Master Guide

  • Advanced Pathfinder Leadership Award (APLA or PSA)

Basic requirements to become a Master Guide

  • 1. Must be a baptized member, in regular standing, of the Seventh-day Adventist Church.
  • 2. Must be above or at least 18 years old on or before April 30, 2024.
  • 3. Must be active in church or any religious programs.

Investiture Program

MMA Pathfinders, and the Master Guide Club of Mindanao Mission Academy hold its Investiture Program last May 13, 2023. The program serves as the culmination to recognize all of their sacrifices during the training. For the Master Guide Trainees, the said ceremony will allow them to take one step higher, from being a Pathfinder to become a full member of the world-wide organization of the Master Guide Club whose main purpose is to carry on the "Salvation" & "Service" to the community.

Beginner's Swimming (Actual)

Stretching, Water entry, Bubbling, Glides, Flatter Kick, Freestyle, Breastroke, Backstroke, and introduction to the Butterfly stroke are the skills that MMA Master Guide Trainees performed during their Beginner's Swimming (Actual) last January 22, 2023 at Johans Swimming Pool, Naawan.

Pathfinder Camping

To experience living away from the comfort zone and to learn some new skills necessary for survival, the MMA Pathfinders held its annual Camping last December 15-18, 2022. While the Pathfinders enjoying the moment as campers, the Master Guide Trainees also serve as the couselor for the Pathfinders.
